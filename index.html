Children & Family

Music encourages activity and creativity, encounters and exchange, the overcoming of inhibitions and the fear of contact. Together, we listen to, explore and invent music, because: “It’s never too early for classical music” (chief conductor Kirill Petrenko).
  • Interactive concerts
  • Family concerts
  • Guided family tours
  • Open Door Day

Adults

Music speaks equally to body, soul and spirit. We want to reach as many people as possible with our education and outreach work – children, young people and adults. What counts for us in all our programmes is that music is a force for development and change. There are opportunities to rediscover (classical) music as well as offers to deepen your understanding of it.
  • Music & research
  • Guided tours
  • Concert introductions
  • Open Door Day

Schools

Our educational offers for childcare centres and schools range from rehearsal and concert visits to workshops and long-term partnerships. The Philharmonie and the orchestra can be explored together as a musical place of learning.
  • KlangKids
  • Open rehearsals
  • youngSTAR Festival
  • Partner school
  • Guided tours for schools

Adults

In the Berliner Philharmoniker’s wide-ranging education programme, we want to offer as many people as possible the chance to experience the music of our orchestra live. To do this, we go out into the city and play and sing at many venues throughout Berlin and the world.
